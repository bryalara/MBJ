ABOUT

Welcome to BRIDGE where our mission is to level the educational and career-related playing fields by increasing accessibility to mentorship regarding both professional and personal matters.We want to focus more on bridging the gap between mentors and mentees who don't have the resources to get ahead in their field of interest. Whether it is learning code from a CS professor/engineer or learning personal experiences from people of color in the technology field, this app will help users grow professionally and personally.
Our creators wanted this website to be an inclusive place for everyone, regardless of their background. Click on the pictures below to connect with each of us!
