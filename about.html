About Us

Welcome to BRIDGE. Our mission is to increase accessibility to mentorship regarding both professional and personal matters. We bridge the gap for mentees who may not have the resources to receive advice in their field of interest. Whether it is learning code from an engineer or hearing incite from people of color in technology fields, this app helps users grow both professionally and personally.
